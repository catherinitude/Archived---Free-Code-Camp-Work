These notes are not meant to be a full documentation of these topics. They were created for me as a reference. Most of the information here is directly from the the site Free Code Camp. From time to time I may have done extra research on an item and included it here.

This was not easy to navigate! I also did not style the information, something I plan on fixing here! I am by no means and expert but I think I can do much better organizing this data on one easy to navigate page after learning what I have.
